Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a service provider?

To find a service provider, you can browse our categories or use the search bar to look for specific services. You can also filter providers based on ratings, reviews, and availability.

How do I book a service?

Booking a service is simple. Once you've found a provider you like, select their profile to view available time slots and services. Choose your preferred option and follow the prompts to confirm your booking.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and secure online payment platforms. You can find the full list of accepted methods during the checkout process.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary depending on the service provider. Please review the specific provider's policy on their profile page before booking. Some services may offer a full refund if canceled within a certain timeframe.

How do I contact customer support?

Our customer support team is here to help! You can reach us via the "Contact Us" page on our website, or by emailing support@dbproject.com. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
